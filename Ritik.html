Ritik Kumar
Where did you interned at ?
IIT BOMBAY
Why did you opt for this internship ?
At the end of my 2nd year I had 2 internships offer one from IITB and other from a startup BotSync Pvt. Ltd. I choose to join IITB as Eklavya is considered as one of the finest research Internship available in India with students participating from IITs and NITs. Secondly, IITB would be the best place to learn and spend summer under guidance of one of best project managers and professor in country.
How will an internship at a Private firm affect my career ?
Internship at a IIT would definitely boost the resume and would give you an upper hand over other candidates while applying for internships or jobs in future.
What skills got you the internship ?
Eklavya does not require anyone to know a lot of things. Some previous projects, a decent pointer and good grasp of c/c++ is sufficient.
Does CGPA matter for this kind of internship ?
CGPA do matter it requires atleast 8.5 cgpa. But it also does have some software quota which doesn't require any cgpa. Students are assigned tasks to complete and are judged on basis of that.
Whom did you approach for the internship ?
A online portal opens up at the end of Feb and then followed by 3 rounds of selection.
Was an insider recommendation required for the internship ?
Yes a recommendation from a professor can help but is not necessary.
When did you start sending out requests ?
February starting
Anything else should know about the internship ?
In my opinion Eklavya is one of best internship opportunity one can look forward to. Being in IITB for two months and working on large scale projects not only gives a lot of skills but also very good exposure and connections. One can learn a lot of things from students from other IITs and NITs. The mentors are really very helpful and are available for most of the time for help and guidance. Students get ample time to learn and work on projects.
