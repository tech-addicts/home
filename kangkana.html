Kangkana
Where did you interned at ?
I interned in University of Calgary, Alberta, Canada via MITACS. It was jointly funded by MITACS (Canada) and MHRD (Ministry of Human Resources & Development), India.
Why did you opt for this internship ?
I opted for this internship becausez Mitacs GRI (Globalink Research Internship) is one of the most prestigious research internships in the world. As I am always interested in pursuing research, so I didn't opt an internship in any private firm/company.
How will an internship at a Private firm affect my career ?
This kind of research internship that I did will greatly effect your resume if you're further interested in pursuing research or if you want to go abroad for further studies, and that's why i did it.
What skills got you the internship ?
I got selected in a project of Experimental Fluid Dynamics, which is my subject of interest. I believe my previous projects (which were related to this one) plus my previous work in Image Processing & Analysis in softwares such as ImageJ, MATLAB & PIVLab got me this internship.
Does CGPA matter for this kind of internship ?
Yes, CGPA highly matters for this kind of internship. MITACS kept 8.5 CGPA as the minimum bar, but the higher your grade point is, it is more likely that you'll be selected. Anyway almost all the research internships will require you to have a moderate to high CGPA.
Whom did you approach for the internship ?
I applied for this one via the portal of MITACS. It's open every year in August - September. You can check the website here for more info: https://www.mitacs.ca/en/programs/globalink/globalink-research-internship
What was the medium of application ?
For this internship, you have to apply through their portal only. They'll contact you regarding any necessary information via your email.
Was an insider recommendation required for the internship ?
Yes, for this one you need to have at least one recommendation letter from one of your teachers/previous project guide. I was recommended by an professor in IIT Guwahati under whom I previously did my summer internship project. It's not "insider recommendation", more of a mandatory one that you need to have to apply.
How many private firms / Universities / government companies did you approach ?
I applied for this one and another government research internship FAST-SF, 2019 via Indian Academy of Science. I got selected in both. But I had to let go of the later since I already went through all the procedures (visa and all) for the former one.
When did you start sending out requests ?
I applied for both the internships in the given timeline, which is usually August - September for MITACS and October - November for FAST-SF if you're planning to do your internship in the summer of next year.
Anything else should know about the internship ?
I wish I could advise you more about internships in private industries/firms. They usually require some inside contacts along with your skills. But since I don't have experience about these internships, I'm not the right person to talk about that. If you want to know more about MITACS Globalink Research Internship, feel free to ask me with your specific questions.
