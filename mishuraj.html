Mishu Raj
Where did you interned at ?
L&T Construction
Why did you opt for this internship ?
Initially, I wasn't sure about doing private job in future. So basically to get an idea of corporate life and the work culture, I opted to go for it. Research hasn't been my interest till now and as far as government organization is concerned, learning is limited especially during internship.
How will an internship at a Private firm affect my career ?
Internship plays a very vital role for getting you in a better shape before you kick-start your career. You get to meet new People, learn from their experiences, and more importantly, you're exposed to the real world and deal with real-life problems.
What skills got you the internship ?
The first criteria to sit for the internship test was to be among the top-10 students of your respective departments. I wasn't that strong technically but my interpersonal skills really helped me to get through the HR round comfortably.
Does CGPA matter for this kind of internship ?
Yeah, CGPA did matter for this internship but it wasn't the sole thing responsible for securing it. If you're low with your CGPA, try improving it as much you can in the upcoming semesters. While doing that focus on developing your soft skills along with other technical skills, it will help you grow in confidence.
Whom did you approach for the internship ?
I got this internship after qualifying the written test and the HR interview when this company visited our campus for placement drive during my 3rd Year.
What was the medium of application ?
Medium of application was through the training and placement cell of our college.
Was an insider recommendation required for the internship ?
No, insider recommendation wasn't required for this internship. If you don't have an insider to recommend you, try getting it through your college alumni who are already working in the companies you're interested to apply in.
How many private firms / Universities / government companies did you approach ?
It was the first company that visited our campus for internship and I got selected in it. So, I didn't approach any other organization after that.
When did you start sending out requests ?
I started sending out requests a month after commencement of my 5th semester though it didn't count in the end.
Anything else should know about the internship ?
Don't depend on theoretical knowledge only. Focus on soft skills. They're very important and they add a touch to your personality which gives a positive impression about you in any interview you face. There's no such thing as right time. The only time you've is now. So start working now and ultimately you'll do something wonderful.
